UAVDE: UAV Distance Estimation dataset


Abstract


The UAV Distance Estimation (UAVDE) dataset is specifically curated for the purpose of UAV target distance estimation. Rather than obtaining the dataset through simulation, all the images and distance measurements in the UAVDE dataset are derived from real-world scenarios. This ensures that the dataset accurately represents the challenges and complexities encountered in actual UAV target distance estimation. The following shows further information,

  • 3,895 pairs of stereo images, each with its corresponding distance.

  • Each individual image is collected at a resolution of $1280*720$ pixels.

  • The horizontal and vertical field of view (FOV) are set at $22^\circ$ and $18^\circ$, respectively.

  • The stereo camera has a baseline distance of $406mm$.

  • To capture the UAV targets accurately, the lenses used have a focal length of $12mm$, and the sensor size is $3.75mm*3.75mm$.
